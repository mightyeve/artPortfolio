Distances between us

Letters to Pittsburgh

I started this project in September of 2020. Due to the Pandemic it has been challenging to interact with my friends and I wanted to contact them. The conventional method would be a email, call, text, or facetime, but isn't more interesting to send a letter? Throughout that time I wrote some letters and made postcards for my special friend. These are the post cards I've made through that time period. Letters remind me of countless stories I've heard when I was little (Kiki's Delivery Service and Balto the Sled Dog). I was also super inspired by Ragnar Kjartansson's "Postcards to Marguerite". I viewed them at my first visit to a museum and it was touching. The walls were filled with postcards with colorful and mesmerizing illustrations. I hope you enjoy my version!
