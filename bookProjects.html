Hello,

This is a project I've been working on since the spring of 2020. It started as an art project for my college class but I wanted to evolve it into something bigger. I love storytelling and the atmosphere of children's books. I think that kids should have a hopeful outlook on the world and I want to communicate to them that the world is a wonderful place to live. Reality is dark and scary, but not 100%. Certainly, the gravity of current events and worldly issues will always remain; however, love and joy outweigh it all in the end of the day. I want to promote of message of self love, love for the Earth, our home, and love for each other.

I now invite you, to a journey into the galaxy.
